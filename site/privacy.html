Privacy Policy

Acceptance of Terms
By registering on the nihilium Minecraft server, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not register.
Data we collect
We only collect data that is essential for Nihilium
  • Player username
  • Player password
  • Player UUID
  • Player settings
  • Player homes
However, the software we use also stores data we do not need or use such as
  • Player IP
  • Player messages
Data usage
Providing service
We use the data we collect to provide features such as homes, configurable mace damage or a configurable player list that require us to store the above mentioned data.
Data storage and security
We store the data we collect securely. We will make sure unauthorized third parties do not have access to this data.
Data sharing
We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. It is up to the player to check when it gets updated. This website is open source on github where the edit history is present.
For questions or concerns, contact us via the information present on the about page
